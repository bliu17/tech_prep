

对称性：

例1:
You have 8 coins, and your opponent has 9 coins. All coins are fair and flipped simulta-neously. What is the probability your opponent obtains more Heads than you do?

Let's divide your opponent's 9 coins into two groups: the **first 8 coins** and the **9th coin**.

Because you and your opponent both flip 8 coins to start, we can compare the number of Heads you get against the number of Heads your opponent gets from their first 8 coins. There are three possible scenarios for this partial comparison:

1. **Your opponent gets more Heads than you in the first 8 coins.** In this case, your opponent has already won. It doesn't matter what happens with their 9th coin.
    
2. **Your opponent gets fewer Heads than you in the first 8 coins.** In this case, your opponent cannot win. Even if their 9th coin is a Head, they will at best tie you, which is not strictly more Heads.
    
3. **You and your opponent get the exact same number of Heads in the first 8 coins.** In this tie-breaker scenario, your opponent will have strictly more Heads than you _if and only if_ their 9th coin lands on Heads (which happens exactly 50% of the time).
    

By the law of symmetry, because you are both flipping 8 fair coins, the probability of Scenario 1 exactly equals the probability of Scenario 2. Let's call this probability $p$. Let the probability of the tie (Scenario 3) be $q$.

Since these three scenarios cover all possibilities for the first 8 coins, their probabilities must sum to 1:

$$2p + q = 1$$

Now, let's calculate the total probability that your opponent wins:

- They win automatically in Scenario 1 (probability $p$).
    
- They win half the time in Scenario 3 (probability $q \times \frac{1}{2}$).
    

Total probability of winning = $p + \frac{q}{2}$

If we take our earlier equation ($2p + q = 1$) and divide both sides by 2, we get:

$$p + \frac{q}{2} = \frac{1}{2}$$



#### 2. 盲人分硬币 (The Blindfolded Coins)

**题目：** 桌子上有 100 枚硬币，其中 10 枚正面朝上，90 枚反面朝上。你被蒙住了双眼，戴着厚手套，无法通过触摸判断正反。请你将硬币分成两堆，使得这两堆中**正面朝上的硬币数量完全一样**。你可以随意翻转硬币。 

**解题思路提示：** 这同样是一个利用“代数逻辑和动作对称”的问题。不需要概率计算，只需从 100 枚中拿出 10 枚作为第一堆，剩下的 90 枚作为第二堆，然后将第一堆的硬币**全部翻转**。

我们可以用非常简单的代数来复盘这个过程。假设在你随机拿出的“第一堆”（10 枚硬币）中，碰巧有 $x$ 枚是正面朝上的。

|**状态**|**第一堆 (10枚)**|**第二堆 (90枚)**|
|---|---|---|
|**分配完成后**|$x$ 枚正面， $10-x$ 枚反面|$10-x$ 枚正面|
|**第一堆翻面后**|**$10-x$ 枚正面**， $x$ 枚反面|**$10-x$ 枚正面** (未触碰)|

#### 3. 不公平的硬币变公平 (Von Neumann Extractor)

**题目：** 你有一枚不公平的硬币，抛出正面的概率是 $p$（$p$ 不等于 $0.5$），抛出反面的概率是 $1-p$。你如何利用这枚硬币，设计出一个概率严格为 $50\%$ 的事件？

**解题思路提示：** 利用连续两次抛掷的对称性。抛两次硬币，出现“正-反”的概率是 $p(1-p)$，出现“反-正”的概率是 $(1-p)p$。两者概率完全相等。
